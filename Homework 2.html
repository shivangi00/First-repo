My Favourite Snack

Banana Chocolate Chip Muffins

These Banana Chocolate Muffins are my personal favourite. These are the easiest and fastest go-to snack when you are famished or have unexpected guests coming over. They are the most delicious hypnotism to your taste buds and are your magical wand to impress anyone and everyone around.

So what are we waiting for?

LET'S GET STARTED! 😄

LIST OF INGREDIENTS:

  • 2 Ripe Bananas
  • 2 Eggs
  • 1 Cup peanut butter
  • 2 Tsp Vanilla
  • 2 Tbsp Honey
  • 1/2 Tsp Baking Powder
  • 4 Chocolate chips each

DIRECTIONS:

  1. Put all the ingredients in a blender. 🍌🥚🧈
  2. Pour the blended mixture in a muffin tin. 🥣
  3. Top with a few chocolate chips. 🍫
  4. Bake at 400F for 8 minutes. 🍪
  5. Take out your muffins when golden brown in colour.

And TOODLES!!! Here you Go! Enjoy their soft and creamy texture with crunchy choco bites and sweet aroma.
HAPPY EATING!!! 😄
